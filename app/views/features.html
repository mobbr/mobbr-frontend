Build your software open-source style

Why

Software projects can become expensive to manage and good developers are hard to find.

How

Develop software with open source mehodology. Pay software developers in line with the amount of their code that ends up in the end-product.

What

Simply use the Mobbr plugin for your favorite version control software. Let Mobbr use the built-in ranking mechanism in software such as Github to divide the project fee among all developers that contributed.

Crowdsource your helpdesk

Why

Helpdesks face the continuous challenge of improving satisfaction targets whilst lowering costs.

How

By simply integrating the Mobbr button those helpdesks can enable crowdsourcing of customer service in a way that is more personal and much more efficient. After all, the crowd never takes a break.

What

Set up a Q&A or open up your existing helpdesk forum to the crowd and use the Mobbr payment button to pledge money to specific questions, categories or tags. In addition, people can reward eachother.
