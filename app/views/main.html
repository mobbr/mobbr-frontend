In the future people will increasingly earn their money through online crowd-sourcing. An any-time any-place economy will emerge. Lets start this new economy now by first making every online participator rewardable. Actually, we find it strange that this is not already the case. Help us make this happen by adding the Mobbr button to your site.

1. Define your crowdsourcing

  • Decide which crowd or community you want to involve with your business
  • Think about how this crowd or community can work for you
  • Determine which participation types should be rewarded
  • Identify possible money streams

2. Create the reward plan

  • Define a static or dynamic ratio that Mobbr should use to distribute rewards
  • Place a Mobbr button for every person or group you want to make rewardable
  • Publish a leaderboard or ranking for content and contributors, for transparency and motivation

3. Let the rewarding begin

  • Explain your community, advertisers and partners how it works
  • Let your users donate or pay eachother
  • Let your advertisers or partners reward you and your community direcly
  • Regurlay pay parts of any income stream on the buttons to create a positive feedback loop
