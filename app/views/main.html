Connect your site
Plugin

To integrate a site it must have a reputation or ranking system. Mobbr divides payments according to that ratio. Easiest way is to install a plugin for your websystem. This can be done by your system administrator.

Find a plugin
HTML metadata

The most flexible way is have your web-designer generate a simple script into the HTML of your site. Upon payment Mobbr will do a callback to your page and find the script.

Metadata integration
API

If your site has an API in which Mobbr can find the contribution statistics for your projects, we can integrate your site from our side. We already did this with Github, Gitlab and Stackexchange.
